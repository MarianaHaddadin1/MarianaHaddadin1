About

I'm a Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering at SMU. My research focuses on second‑life lithium‑ion batteries, battery aging and health modeling, and AI‑driven Battery Management Systems (BMS). I aim to advance intelligent energy storage through digital‑twin methods, data‑driven modeling, and rigorous lab validation.

Keywords: second‑life battery reuse, age‑aware charging, lithium‑plating risk, EIS/HPPC testing, digital twins for BMS.

Research Snapshot

Second‑Life Batteries for Off‑Grid & EV Charging

Assessing feasibility and control strategies for integrating retired eVTOL/EV cells into stationary storage and EV fast‑charging buffers.

AI for BMS

Machine‑learning‑assisted SoH/SoC estimation and aging‑aware control policies; integrating simplified electrochemical models with data‑driven predictors.

Cold‑Temperature Behavior & Pre‑Heating

Modeling and testing pulsed pre‑heating for high‑power vs. high‑energy cells, analyzing impedance and voltage signatures across SOC/temperature.

Battery Aging & End‑of‑Life Prediction

Multi‑constraint frameworks for end‑of‑life in safety‑critical aviation batteries, balancing performance, safety, and cost.
